Privacy Policy

App: alx-post-app · Last updated: 2 June 2026

alx-post-app ("the app") is a personal tool used by its sole owner to produce short educational personal-finance videos and upload them to the owner's own TikTok account. The app runs locally on the owner's computer. This policy explains what data it handles and how.

1. Who this concerns

The app is single-user: it is operated by, and connects only to, the TikTok account of its owner. It does not serve other end users and does not act on behalf of third parties.

2. Data the app accesses

  • TikTok authorization tokens (access token, refresh token) and the account identifier (open_id), obtained through TikTok's official OAuth flow when the owner authorizes the app.
  • Basic account info (user.info.basic) used only to identify the authorizing account.
  • Video files and captions created by the owner and uploaded to the owner's TikTok account.

3. How data is used

Tokens are used solely to call the TikTok Content Posting API (video.upload) to send finished videos to the owner's TikTok drafts, which the owner then reviews and publishes manually inside the TikTok app. The app does not use the data for any other purpose.

4. Storage and security

Tokens are stored locally in an environment file on the owner's machine and are never committed to source control. They are not transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

5. Data sharing

No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. The only external service contacted with this data is TikTok itself.

6. Data retention and deletion

Tokens persist locally only as long as needed and can be revoked at any time by the owner from their TikTok account settings, or by deleting the local environment file. Revoking authorization immediately stops all access.
